xposed框架，技术圈资深手机玩家们，都在用的一款手机框架。安装了这个框架后，就可以使用大神们开发的各种插件，类似于前几天推荐的“油猴脚本”。
xposed框架下的插件非常多，下载无水印视频、自动抢红包、视频去广告、自动打卡、修改运动步数、消息防撤回、改手机设备码... 该有的都有。
“太极框架”和XP框架类似，免ROOT，各种高级功能和玩法都有。

Xposed framework, a mobile phone framework used by senior mobile phone players in the technical circle. After installing this framework, you can use various plug-ins developed by the gods, similar to the "oil monkey script" recommended a few days ago.
There are many plug-ins in xposed framework, such as downloading non watermark video, automatically grabbing red packets, video advertising, automatic clock in, modifying movement steps, message anti withdrawal, changing mobile device code, etc.
"Taiji framework" is similar to XP framework, free of root, and has various advanced functions and playing methods.
Free to a few pictures, you feel: XP framework official website: https://repo.xposed.info/module-overview
Official website of Taiji framework: https://www.taichi-app.com/#/index
Official website of oil monkey script: https://greasyfork.org/zh-CN As we all know, the server is abroad, so sometimes it may be slow to open. All of the above are free and better than the software you pay for.
